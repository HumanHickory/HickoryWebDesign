Web Optimization Services

Our web optimization services complement new or pre-existing websites, allowing you to get the most out of your online experience.

Search Engine Optimization, or SEO, helps increase the exposure of your website. When potential clients search for specific words, your website is more likely to be on the first page of results. A key to using this optimization process is to know your clientele, which is where Web Analytics comes into play. Together, we can make your website thrive using these techniques!

Web Analytics
Web analytics is the ability to track what is happening on your site. From who is visiting to what device they are on, you can make changes to your site to fit your audience needs. Create an account and add web analytics to a pre-existing site and have a 1-hour training seminar for up to 5 people. Choose any web analytics program you like, such as Google Analytics and Piwik.
Web Exposure
Number of visitors is often a Key Performance Indicator (KPI) of a website; the more visitors you have, the more opportunities you have to convert them to customers! Create an account and add extensions like Google AdWords or Facebook Pixel to your website. Both of these programs, as well as a variety of other programs, help new clients find your website easier and more often. Pair with a web analytics service for best results.
