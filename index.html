Web Design and Optimization

Hickory Web Designs is a new company based in Del Rio, TX whose goal is to make small business easy. Using Knowledge gained through university education, certifications and experience, Hickory Web Designs builds simple, managable websites for small business owners looking to grow their profits while benefiting their communities.

All websites are built using modern website design programming that is compatible on all web browsers, such as Google Chrome, Safari, Internet Explorer, Edge, and Firefox, as well as all platforms, including computer and mobile devices. This means that ALL your customers will be able to view and access your website flawlessly.

Additionally, Hickory Web Designs also provides services such as analytics tool integration and advertisement additions. This means that, in addition to having a new website built, we can help optimize your site as well!

Office Professionals

Despite the name, Hickory Web Designs does not just specialize in web development. We can also help train you or your staff on Microsoft Office productivity and presentation software, such as Word or Excel.
